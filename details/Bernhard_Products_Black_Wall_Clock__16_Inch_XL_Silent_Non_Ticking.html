Stylish and Reliable 16-Inch Black Wall Clock

This 16-inch extra large black wall clock is the perfect blend of functionality and design. Its bold black Arabic numerals on a white face make it easy to read from any distance or angle, making it ideal for use in homes, offices, classrooms, gyms, or other spaces. The clock is built with precision quartz movement, ensuring accurate timekeeping, while the silent non-ticking mechanism provides a smooth, quiet experience without any disruptive ticking sounds.

The sweeping second hand adds a visually appealing movement that enhances the overall aesthetic of the clock. It features a sturdy plastic frame with a 1.75-inch depth for durability and a glass cover that not only protects the dial but also makes cleaning a breeze. This design is suitable for all ages, including adults, seniors, and children, and is crafted to be both easy to use and long-lasting. The clock requires a single AA battery, which is not included, and comes with a 1-year manufacturer warranty for added peace of mind.
