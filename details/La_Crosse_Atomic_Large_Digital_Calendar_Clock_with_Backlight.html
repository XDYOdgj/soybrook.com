La Crosse Atomic Large Digital Calendar Clock with Backlight

A must-have for anyone who values precision and clarity in daily life.

Clear and Easy to Read

This clock is designed with extra-large, easy-to-read digits that ensure visibility even for those with visual impairments or memory loss. The bold display makes it simple to stay on top of time and date without confusion.

Adjustable Backlight for Any Lighting

With a built-in adjustable backlight offering Hi/Low/Off settings, this clock adapts to different lighting conditions. Whether it's nighttime or a bright room, the backlight ensures you can always tell the time with ease.

Auto Adjust Time with Atomic Signal

Thanks to the advanced Atomic time signal, this clock automatically updates time and calendar information without the need for manual adjustments. It stays accurate to the second, giving you reliable timekeeping all day long.

Simple Setup and Customizable Display

Setting up this clock is simple, allowing you to choose your preferred time zone quickly. The display can be customized to show weekday, date, seconds, or indoor temperature, making it a versatile and functional addition to your space.

Versatile Placement Options

Easily mount this clock on a wall or place it on a desk. Its modern and elegant design complements any interior, from minimalist setups to traditional décor.

Power Options for Flexibility

This clock is powered by either batteries or an AC adapter, giving you the flexibility to choose between on-demand and continuous use. It’s designed to work seamlessly in any environment.

Perfect for both homes and offices, this digital clock combines functionality with a sleek, user-friendly design to enhance your daily routine and provide accurate timekeeping.
